Research

Secure and resilient energy infrastructure.

The lab works across power systems, computer networks, control systems, data analytics, and operational technology security.

Areas

SCADA and OT Security

Intrusion detection, protocol-aware monitoring, situational awareness, and cyber-event analysis for grid control environments.

DER and Inverter Security

Cybersecurity and resilience for distributed energy resources, inverter controls, grid-edge devices, and DER communication protocols.

Microgrid Resilience

Methods and testbeds for protecting microgrid-integrated distribution systems under cyber and physical disturbances.

AI for Grid Defense

Data-driven techniques for anomaly detection, risk assessment, attack impact analysis, and automated decision support.

Current Project Tracks

CyDERMS

Cybersecurity and resiliency of distributed energy resources and microgrids-integrated distribution systems.

Protocol Conversion

Models and tools for DER communication, including SunSpec Modbus, DNP3, and interoperable gateway workflows.

Datasets

Curated datasets for DER, microgrid, and anomaly detection research that support reproducible experimentation.
