Cybersecurity for the modern power grid.

PowerCyber Laboratory studies resilient, secure, and intelligent energy systems, with a focus on cyber-physical security for power grids, distributed energy resources, microgrids, operational technology, and critical infrastructure.

Power systems meet cyber defense. Secure control, trusted data, resilient operation, and testbeds for real-world energy infrastructure.

Research Themes

Grid Cybersecurity

Detection, analysis, and mitigation of cyber attacks against power system monitoring, communication, and control networks.

DERs and Microgrids

Security and resilience for distributed energy resources, inverter-rich systems, and microgrid-integrated distribution networks.

Cyber-Physical Testbeds

Experimental platforms that connect simulation, communication protocols, and operational scenarios for reproducible research.

Featured Project

CyDERMS is one of the lab's major research efforts, focused on cybersecurity and resiliency of distributed energy resources and microgrids-integrated distribution systems.
